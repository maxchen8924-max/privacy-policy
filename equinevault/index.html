EquineVault Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Data Collection

EquineVault does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — horse profiles, health records, veterinary visits, farrier appointments, vaccinations, deworming logs, and dental records — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's built-in data framework (SwiftData).

No Accounts, No Cloud

EquineVault requires no user account, no sign-up, and no cloud service. There are no servers, no databases, and no third-party analytics. Your data never leaves your device.

Location Data

EquineVault does not access, collect, or transmit your location data.

Photos

If you choose to add photos to horse profiles, EquineVault accesses your photo library only with your explicit permission. Photos are stored locally within the app's data container and are never uploaded or shared.

Export & Backup

Data export (PDF/CSV) and backup features save files locally to your device or share them only through iOS system sharing sheets you control. Exported data contains only what you choose to include.

Children's Privacy

EquineVault is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Third-Party Services

EquineVault uses no third-party services, SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics tools.
