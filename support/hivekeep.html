📋 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a hive?
Tap the + button on the Horses tab to create a new horse record. Fill in the details and save.

How do I back up my data?
Go to More → Backup → Export. This creates a JSON file you can save to iCloud Drive or your computer via the Files app.

How do I restore from a backup?
Go to More → Backup → Import, then select your previously exported file.

Does HiveKeep work offline?
Yes. HiveKeep works entirely offline. No internet connection is needed for any feature.

Is my data private?
Absolutely. HiveKeep stores all data on your device only. There are no accounts, no cloud servers, and no tracking. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I change the language?
Go to More → Language. HiveKeep supports English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Brazilian Portuguese, and Traditional Chinese.
