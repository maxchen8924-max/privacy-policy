HuntCache Support

HuntCache 2.0 — Offline, encrypted, yours.

Quick answer: HuntCache is 100% offline. There is no account, no server, and no cloud sync. If something is wrong, it's on your device, and we can help you fix it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my vault locked?

HuntCache locks automatically after 90 seconds in the background (configurable in Settings). Unlock with Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode.

I forgot my device passcode. Can I recover my data?

No. Your encrypted vault key is stored in the Secure Enclave and protected by your passcode. Without it, the data is unreadable — by design. This is the same security model as Apple Pay.

How do I change the language?

Open the Vault tab → Settings → Language. Pick your language and restart the app. Eight languages are supported: English, English (UK), Deutsch, Español, Français, 日本語, Português (Brasil), and 繁體中文.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Go to the Vault tab → Export. You can export as CSV or PDF, with coordinates either masked (county-level, ~11 km) or exact. License number and region fields are available for agency submissions.

Does HuntCache sync with iCloud?

No. By design, all data stays on your device. No cloud, no sync, no server access. If you switch devices, you would need to manually export and re-import.

Why are coordinates showing as dots?

Coordinate masking is on by default (Settings → Mask coordinates). Tap the eye icon on the map to temporarily reveal them. This protects your spots from shoulder-surfers.

How do I reset the vault?

Go to Vault tab → Settings → Reset the vault key. This will make all encrypted coordinates and notes permanently unreadable. Your catches, gear, and species will remain.

Which species are included?

HuntCache ships with 40+ common North American and European game and fish species. You can add custom species from the Logs tab.
