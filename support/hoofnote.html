Frequently Asked Questions

Does HoofNote require an internet connection?

No. HoofNote is designed for barns and stables where connectivity is unreliable. Every core feature works offline.

What can I track in the app?

You can record horse profiles, health scores, vet visits, farrier schedules, vaccinations, deworming, and dental records, including follow-up dates for recurring care.

Can I export my records?

Yes. HoofNote can generate PDF exports so you can share clean records with your vet, farrier, or boarding facility.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad using Apple system storage. We do not collect or transmit your data.

Can you recover my data if I delete the app?

No. Because HoofNote keeps everything on your device, we do not have server-side backups or access to your records.
