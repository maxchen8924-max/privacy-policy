Getting Started

EquineVault is a private, offline horse health log. No account needed — just open the app and start adding your horses.

Features

  • Horse Profiles — Name, breed, color, gender, date of birth, microchip, registration, barn name, and photo
  • Health Score — 1–10 scale health rating with color indicators
  • Veterinary Visits — Date, vet, reason, cost, diagnosis, and next due date
  • Farrier Records — Shoeing, trimming, farrier name, cost, and scheduling
  • Vaccinations — Vaccine name, batch number, vet, cost, and booster reminders
  • Deworming — Product, dosage, cost, and next due date
  • Dental Records — Floating, extractions, dentist, and cost tracking
  • Schedule View — Calendar of all upcoming and overdue events
  • Due Reminders — See what's overdue at a glance
  • Language Switching — 8 languages available in Settings
  • PDF/CSV Export — Share records with your vet or farrier

Frequently Asked Questions

Does EquineVault need an internet connection? No. All data is stored locally on your device. No cloud, no account, no internet required.
Can I share records with my vet? Yes. Use the Export feature to generate a PDF summary or CSV data file, then share via iOS sharing.
Is my data safe if I lose my phone? We recommend using the Backup feature to save a copy of your data. You can also export periodically.
How do I change the language? Go to the More (⚙️) tab → Language and select your preferred language. The app updates instantly.
How do I add a photo to a horse? When editing a horse, tap the photo area to choose an image from your photo library.
