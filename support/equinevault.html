Frequently Asked Questions

Does EquineVault require an internet connection?

No. EquineVault is built for stables, pastures, and trail rides where connectivity is unreliable or absent. Every core feature works offline.

What can I track for each horse?

Profile (name, breed, coat, sex, registration, microchip, barn, owner), a 1–10 health score, vet visits, farrier appointments, vaccinations, deworming, dental work, follow-up dates, photos, and free-form notes.

How do I switch the app's language?

Tap More, then Language, then choose from English, English (UK), German, French, Spanish, Japanese, Brazilian Portuguese, or Traditional Chinese. The whole app changes instantly — no restart needed.

Can I export my records?

Yes. EquineVault generates clean PDFs you can share with your vet, insurer, or new yard manager via AirDrop, email, or any iOS share sheet target. The PDF is created entirely on your device.

Where is my data stored?

All data lives locally on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's SwiftData framework. We never see your records. Nothing leaves your device unless you explicitly share a PDF.

Can you recover my data if I delete the app?

No. Because EquineVault keeps everything on your device only, we have no server-side backups. Use the Files app to back up your data, or rely on your iCloud iOS device backup.

Do I have to pay every month?

No. EquineVault is a one-time purchase. No subscription, no in-app purchases, no upsell, no ads.

Does EquineVault work on iPad?

Yes. EquineVault is optimised for iPhone and iPad, including the iPad sidebar layout for larger screens.
