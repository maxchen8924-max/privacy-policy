HuntCache Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 30, 2026 | Last Updated: April 9, 2026

Summary: HuntCache collects zero personal data. Your spots and logs stay on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, no ads, no tracking. Your secret spots die with your phone.

1. Introduction

HuntCache ("the App") is an offline hunting and fishing log application for iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection

HuntCache does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or ad-related tracking
  • No cookies or web tracking technologies
  • No third-party analytics SDKs

3. Data Storage

All data you enter (harvest records, catch logs, and settings) is stored exclusively on your device in a local SQLite database. GPS coordinates are encrypted with AES-256-CBC before storage. Other record fields (species, weight, notes, etc.) and photos are stored unencrypted on-device. No data ever leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export.

4. Location Data

HuntCache may request access to your device's GPS location to allow you to tag catch and harvest locations. Location data is:

  • Stored only on your device in encrypted form
  • Never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Only collected when you actively choose to tag a location
  • Completely under your control to delete at any time

5. Photos and Camera

HuntCache may request access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to add photos to your records. Photos are re-encoded on save to remove EXIF metadata (including embedded GPS coordinates). Photos are stored locally on your device and never uploaded to any server. Photos are not encrypted.

6. Notifications

HuntCache may send local push notifications for reminders (e.g., backup reminders, season-end alerts). Notifications are disabled by default and only enabled when you turn them on in Settings. These notifications are generated entirely on your device — no remote push servers are used.

7. Export

The App provides CSV and PDF export features. Export files are created locally on your device and shared through your device's native sharing system.

8. Third-Party Services

HuntCache does not connect to any third-party servers, analytics services, or advertising networks. The App uses only platform-native APIs (system maps, camera, GPS) provided by iOS and Android. No third-party tracking SDKs are included. The App operates entirely offline — core features require no internet connection.

9. Children's Privacy

HuntCache does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

10. Data Retention and Deletion

Retention: Your data is retained on your device for as long as you keep the App installed. There is no automatic expiration or remote deletion. The encryption key for GPS coordinates is stored in the App's local storage on your device; if you uninstall the App, this key is permanently lost and encrypted coordinates cannot be recovered.

Deletion: You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Individual records and photos can be deleted within the App at any time
  • All App data is completely removed by uninstalling HuntCache from your device
  • No data remains on any server after deletion, as no server-side data exists
  • There is no account data to request deletion of, since no accounts are created

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
