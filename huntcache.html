HuntCache Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 30, 2026 | Last Updated: March 30, 2026

Summary: HuntCache collects zero personal data. Your spots and logs stay on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, no ads, no tracking. Your secret spots die with your phone.

1. Introduction

HuntCache ("the App") is an offline hunting and fishing log application for iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection

HuntCache does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or ad-related tracking
  • No cookies or web tracking technologies
  • No third-party analytics SDKs

3. Data Storage

All data you enter (harvest records, catch logs, photos, and settings) is stored exclusively on your device using a local encrypted SQLite database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export your data.

4. Location Data

HuntCache may request access to your device's GPS location to allow you to tag catch and harvest locations. Location data is:

  • Stored only on your device in encrypted form
  • Never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Only collected when you actively choose to tag a location
  • Completely under your control to delete at any time

5. Photos and Camera

HuntCache may request access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to add photos to your records. These photos are stored locally and never uploaded to any server.

6. Notifications

HuntCache may request permission to send local push notifications for reminders. These notifications are generated entirely on your device.

7. Export

The App provides CSV and PDF export features. Export files are created locally on your device and shared through your device's native sharing system.

8. Third-Party Services

HuntCache does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. The App operates entirely offline.

9. Children's Privacy

HuntCache does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
