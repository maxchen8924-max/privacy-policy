HoofNote — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

TL;DR: HoofNote collects absolutely no data. Your information stays on your device, period.

1. Data Collection

HoofNote does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data you enter into the app — horse profiles, health records, vet visits, farrier schedules, vaccination logs, and photos — remains exclusively on your device.

We do not have access to your data. Period.

2. No Third-Party Services

HoofNote contains zero third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, or tracking services. The app makes no network requests whatsoever.

  • No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No crash reporting (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No social login
  • No cloud sync

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework on your device. Data is encrypted when your device is locked (if you have a passcode enabled). Data is never uploaded, synced, or shared.

4. Permissions

HoofNote requests the following device permissions:

  • Photos Library (optional): Used solely to attach photos of your horses and health records. Photos are stored on your device only and are never uploaded.
  • Notifications (optional): Used solely to remind you of upcoming vet visits, farrier appointments, and vaccinations. Notification scheduling is handled entirely on-device.

No other permissions are requested.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete individual records or all data at any time via the in-app Settings screen. Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.

We have no backup of your data because we never had it in the first place.

6. Children's Privacy

HoofNote does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, this policy is inherently satisfied.

7. International Users

Since all data stays on your device and no data crosses borders, this privacy policy applies equally to all users worldwide regardless of jurisdiction.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy (which we have no reason to — our commitment to zero data collection is foundational), we will update this page and the in-app privacy label. Changes will only apply to future app versions and will never retroactively collect data.
