HuntCache — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2026 | Last Updated: April 27, 2026

TL;DR: HuntCache collects absolutely no data. Your spots, logs, and coordinates stay on your device — encrypted. No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, no ads, no tracking. Your secret spots die with your phone.

1. Data Collection

HuntCache does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting SDKs
  • No advertising or ad-related tracking
  • No cookies, web beacons, or tracking technologies
  • No third-party analytics frameworks

2. Data Storage & Encryption

All data you enter (hunting spots, fishing logs, gear records, and settings) is stored exclusively on your device in a local database. GPS coordinates are encrypted with AES-GCM-256 before storage; the encryption key is held in the device Secure Enclave and gated by your passcode. Other record fields (species, weight, notes) and gear photos are stored unencrypted on-device. No data ever leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export.

3. Location Data

HuntCache requests access to your device GPS to mark hunting and fishing spots on the map. Location data is:

  • Encrypted with AES-GCM-256 and stored only on your device
  • Never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Only collected when you actively choose to mark a location
  • Completely under your control to delete at any time

4. Photos & Camera

Photos are processed on save to strip EXIF metadata (including embedded GPS coordinates) before local storage. Photos never leave your device.

5. Biometric Authentication

HuntCache uses Face ID or Touch ID to secure access to the vault. Biometric data is never stored or transmitted — authentication is handled entirely by the iOS Secure Enclave. HuntCache only receives a pass/fail result.

6. Export

CSV and PDF export files are created locally on your device and shared through iOS native sharing. Coordinate precision is user-controlled (exact or county-level masking). Nothing is sent over the network.

7. Third-Party Services

Zero third-party SDKs, analytics, or ad networks. Only platform-native iOS APIs (Maps, Camera, GPS, Keychain, CryptoKit, LocalAuthentication). Operates entirely offline.

8. Children's Privacy

HuntCache does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. No data from any user of any age is collected.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

Data stays on your device for as long as the App is installed. The encryption key is stored in the Secure Enclave — removing your device passcode or uninstalling the App permanently loses the key and encrypted coordinates cannot be recovered. Individual spots, records, and photos can be deleted at any time. Uninstalling removes all data.
