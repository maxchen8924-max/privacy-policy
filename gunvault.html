IronVault Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 30, 2026 | Last Updated: April 9, 2026

Summary: IronVault collects zero personal data. Your collection stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, no ads, no tracking.

1. Introduction

IronVault ("the App"), developed by longchen8924 ("Developer"), is a personal collection management application for iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection

IronVault does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or ad-related tracking
  • No cookies or web tracking technologies
  • No third-party analytics SDKs

3. Data Storage

All data you enter (item records, serial numbers, photos, and settings) is stored exclusively on your device using a local SQLite database. IronVault does not upload your data to our servers. If you enable encrypted device backups at the operating-system level (e.g., iCloud Backup), iOS may include app data in those backups. You can manage which apps are included in device backups through your device's Settings.

4. Photos and Camera

IronVault may request access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to photograph your collection items and serial numbers. These photos are stored locally and never uploaded to any server.

5. Biometric Authentication

IronVault may use Face ID or fingerprint authentication to protect access to your data. Biometric data is handled entirely by your device's operating system. We do not access, store, or transmit any biometric information.

6. Backup and Export

The App provides encrypted backup and export features:

  • Backup files are created locally on your device
  • Backups are encrypted using a password you set
  • We do not store or have access to your backup password
  • Sharing is done through your device's native sharing system

7. Third-Party Services

IronVault does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. The App does not connect to any external servers for its core functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

IronVault does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

9. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data at all times:

  • Individual items can be deleted within the App at any time
  • All App data can be completely removed by uninstalling IronVault from your device
  • No data remains on any server after deletion, as no server-side data exists

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
