HiveKeep Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Data Collection

HiveKeep does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data you enter — hive records, inspection notes, photos, and settings — remains exclusively on your device.

No Accounts Required

HiveKeep has no sign-up, no login, and no cloud sync. There are no user accounts, and we have no way to identify you personally.

No Third-Party Services

HiveKeep contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. There are no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

Photos

Photo access is used solely to attach images of your hives and inspections to your records. Photos are stored only on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

Backup & Export

The backup and export features create files that you choose to save or share. The content of these files is entirely under your control. We do not have access to your backup data.

Local Notifications

If you enable reminders, HiveKeep schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are processed entirely on-device and are not sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

HiveKeep is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.
