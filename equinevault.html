EquineVault Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 30, 2026 | Last Updated: March 30, 2026

Summary: EquineVault collects zero personal data. All your horse health records stay on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, no ads, no tracking.

1. Introduction

EquineVault ("the App") is a horse health record management application for iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection

EquineVault does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or ad-related tracking
  • No cookies or web tracking technologies
  • No third-party analytics SDKs

3. Data Storage

All data you enter (horse profiles, health records, vaccination schedules, photos, and settings) is stored exclusively on your device using a local SQLite database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or back up your data.

4. Photos and Camera

EquineVault may request access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to photograph your horses and health documents. These photos are stored locally and never uploaded to any server.

5. Notifications

EquineVault may request permission to send local push notifications for vaccination, farrier, and Coggins test reminders. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any remote server communication.

6. Backup and Export

The App provides backup and PDF export features:

  • Backup files are created locally on your device
  • Backups may be encrypted using a password you set
  • We do not store or have access to your backup password
  • Sharing is done through your device's native sharing system

7. Third-Party Services

EquineVault does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. The App operates entirely offline.

8. Children's Privacy

EquineVault does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
