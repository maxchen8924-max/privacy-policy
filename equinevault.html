EquineVault — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

TL;DR: EquineVault collects absolutely no data. Your horse health records stay on your device, period.

1. Data Collection

EquineVault does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information you enter into the app — horse profiles, vet visits, farrier appointments, vaccinations, deworming records, dental records, photos, and notes — remains exclusively on your device.

We do not have access to your data. Period.

2. No Third-Party Services

EquineVault contains zero third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, or tracking services. The app makes no network requests whatsoever.

  • No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No crash reporting (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No social login
  • No cloud sync
  • No accounts

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework on your device. Data is encrypted when your device is locked (if you have a passcode enabled). Data is never uploaded, synced, or shared.

4. Permissions

EquineVault requests the following device permissions:

  • Photos Library (optional): Used solely to attach photos of your horses to their records. Photos are stored on your device only and are never uploaded.
  • Camera (optional): Used solely to take new photos of your horses inside the app. Photos are stored on your device only.
  • Notifications (optional): Used solely to remind you of upcoming vet visits, farrier appointments, vaccinations, deworming, and dental work. Reminder scheduling is handled entirely on-device.
  • Calendars (optional): Used only if you choose to add an appointment to your iOS Calendar. EquineVault never reads your existing calendar entries.

No other permissions are requested. You can decline any permission and continue to use the app normally.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete individual horses or records at any time from inside the app, or wipe everything by uninstalling. Uninstalling EquineVault removes all data permanently from your device.

We have no backup of your data because we never had it in the first place.

6. Data Export

EquineVault lets you export your records as a PDF you can hand to your vet, insurer, or new barn manager. The PDF is generated entirely on your device and only goes anywhere if you choose to share it through the iOS share sheet.

7. Children's Privacy

EquineVault does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, this policy is inherently satisfied.

8. International Users

Since all data stays on your device and no data crosses borders, this privacy policy applies equally to all users worldwide regardless of jurisdiction.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy (which we have no reason to — our commitment to zero data collection is foundational), we will update this page and the in-app privacy label. Changes will only apply to future app versions and will never retroactively collect data.
